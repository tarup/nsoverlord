Sekuntikellot on niin nähty. NSOverlord ja sen virtuaaliset ajastimet tekevät roller derbyn toimitsijan työn helpoksi. Sovellus sisältää kaksi ajastinta Jam Timer ja Penalty Box Timer -positioihin. Box Timer voi valita kolmesta eri layoutista mieleisen: kaksi blokkeria, jammerit tai kaikki kuusi penkkiä. Jam Timer voi lisäksi merkitä timeoutit ja official review:t.

README/INSTRUCTIONS/HOW TO USE/HOW IT WORKS/WHAT IS IT

Jam Timer:

In Jam Timer you see three clocks: periodclock under the periodcounter, jam clock big in the middle of the circle and between jams clock under that one.

Starting the timer (clicking start) will start the between jams timer. After 30 seconds the first jam and the period will officially start on the Jam Starting whistle. 

The period clock does not stop between Jams unless a timeout is called or you stop the timers (in case of emergency, while practicing etc.).



"A period starts on the Jam-Starting Whistle of the first Jam for that period. The period clock does not stop between Jams unless a timeout is called. If the period clock reaches zero before the next Jam has started, the period ends at that moment; otherwise, the period ends at the conclusion of the final Jam for the period."
